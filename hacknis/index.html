About Us

Welcome to Emancipation, a passionate team of young students from Nazarbayev International School (NIS) dedicated to environmental awareness and change. We believe in the power of youth to make a positive impact on the world and are committed to addressing environmental challenges. Our mission is to raise awareness about ecological issues, advocate for sustainable practices, and work towards a greener, cleaner planet. Join us on our journey to create a better future for generations to come.
