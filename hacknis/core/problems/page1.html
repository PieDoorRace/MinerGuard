Ecology Problems

Ozone Depletion

Ozone depletion is a critical environmental issue caused by the release of ozone-depleting substances like CFCs. This leads to the thinning of the protective ozone layer, exposing us to harmful UV rays.

Carbon Monoxide Pollution

Carbon monoxide is a dangerous pollutant from incomplete fossil fuel combustion. It poses health risks and contributes to climate change. Sources include vehicle emissions and industry.

Nitrogen Dioxide Emissions

Nitrogen dioxide is released from fossil fuel burning, causing respiratory issues and smog formation. Major sources include car engines and power plants.

Methane Emissions

Methane is a potent greenhouse gas, contributing to climate change. It's released from various sources, including agriculture, livestock, and landfills, and leads to global warming.
